Introduction to Calgary

Located in the province of Alberta and the gateway to the US, Calgary is one of the key cities of Canada. This city is accessible to every resource including the highway and the international airport. It is ranked third in the world's liveable cities. Home of Calgary stampede and bordered by high hills in every direction, you will wonder how life is over here. Having oil and natural gas production makes this city more economically stable. Many offices and warehouses are a key source of employment for most of the people. Its land is also recognized as one of the most productive in terms of agriculture. Not only that, but it also has many more to offer to the people like Stampede every year.

Calgary is third most populated cities after Toronto and Montreal plus having access to nature makes this city even beautiful.

Calgary Stampede

One of the world famous fair which is visited by lot of people, Calgary Stampede is one of the go to place shile visiting Canada. From food to entertainment you will find it it has a lot ot offer to the visitors. You won't feel bored and can find more interesting things about native and lifestyles. Started back in 1886, as an agricultural fair, it has continued to gain more popularity and attract more people every year and now has become an centre of attraction.The centre of attraction are Barrel Racing ,Cowboys Rangerland Derby, Bell Grandstand show, Stampede Parade and many more.
